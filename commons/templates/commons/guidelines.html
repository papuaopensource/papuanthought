Community Guidelines

These guidelines exist to keep The Papuan Thought Project a respectful and constructive space.

Write thoughtfully

Essays should encourage reflection, storytelling, analysis, and meaningful discussion. Quality over quantity.

Respectful discussion

Disagreement is allowed and welcome. Harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks are not.

No spam or self-promotion

Comments and essays should contribute value to the community, not promote external services.

Cultural sensitivity

Respect local knowledge, oral traditions, and community memory. Do not misrepresent Papuan culture.
